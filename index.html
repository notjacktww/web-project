Clara’s Signature Ratio

  • 2 parts vodka
  • 1 part coffee liqueur
  • 1 part fresh espresso
  • ½ part simple syrup (to taste)

Shake hard with ice → fine strain → garnish with 3 coffee beans (health, wealth, happiness!).

The house recipe

  1. Pull a fresh, hot espresso shot (or two). Let it sit 1–2 min to reduce excess steam.
  2. Fill a shaker with ice. Add 2 oz vodka, 1 oz coffee liqueur, 1 oz espresso, and ¼–½ oz simple syrup.
  3. Shake like you mean it for 15–20 seconds to build that velvety foam.
  4. Fine strain into a chilled coupe. Garnish with three coffee beans.

Pro tip: Cold espresso can mute foam—shaking while still slightly warm helps crema.

Make it your own

  • Less sweet: Skip syrup entirely.
  • Richer: Add a barspoon of chocolate bitters.
  • No espresso machine? Use strong cold brew concentrate.
  • Zero-proof: Swap in non-alcoholic vodka & coffee liqueur.

FAQ (aka friends keep asking…)

Can you make it decaf?

Yep—use decaf espresso or a decaf concentrate. Texture stays great.

Shaken or stirred?

Always shaken. You want micro-foam and dilution for that signature texture.

What vodka?

Clean and neutral works best—let the coffee shine.
